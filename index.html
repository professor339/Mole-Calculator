This online tool helps to calculate the amount of sample you need for prepare ( for weighing ) any solid compound..

For Example:- If you want a prepare 0.01 M of NaOH in 3 ml of water, then Just put the values in the boxes including the molecular weight of NaOH ( i.e. 40 ) and click on submit to know the amount of NaOH you need to weigh for preparing your sample for 3 ml of water.
